Refund & Exchange Policy

Regione Siciliana – Your Audio Destination offers you a risk-free shopping experience. If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect item, or change your mind, you may request an exchange or refund within fourteen (14) days of:

  • The date of purchase in one of our physical stores (with proof of purchase).
  • The date you received the product for online orders placed at regionesicilianait.com.

The 14-day period applies to all audio equipment and accessories purchased from us.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

To take advantage of this policy, please:

  1. Log into your account at regionesicilianait.com or contact the store where you purchased.
  2. Within 14 days, submit your request via our Returns & Exchanges page or email support@regionesicilianait.com.
  3. Ship the product back (in original packaging, with all accessories) using a trackable carrier.
  4. Once we receive and inspect the item, we’ll process your refund or ship a replacement within 14 days.

Return Requirements

To qualify for a full refund or exchange, the item must be:

  • Unused and in its original condition, including box, manuals, and all accessories.
  • Returned via an accredited carrier with tracking—return shipping costs are the customer’s responsibility unless the item was defective or shipped incorrectly.
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt).
