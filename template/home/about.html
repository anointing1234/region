Frequently Asked Questions
What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 30-day return and exchange policy on all audio products. If you’re not satisfied, simply return your item in original condition and packaging within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. See our Return Policy page for details.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, we’ll send you a tracking link via email. You can also view your order status in your account under “My Orders.” For any issues, reach out to us via Customer Support.

How do I contact your support team?

Our audio specialists are available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM CET. Email us at support@regionsiciliana.com or use the live chat widget at the bottom right of the page.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times depend on your location and selected carrier. You can review all options and costs during checkout or on our Shipping Information page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.

How do I apply a discount or promo code?

During checkout, enter your code in the “Promo Code” field and click “Apply.” Your total will update to reflect the discount. Only one code can be used per order.
